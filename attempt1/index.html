BLAH is the app that makes you WANT to achieve your daily fitness goals!


Make Walking a Game!

BLAH throws you into the mysterious world of SPLAH as a brand new runner. Pick your running partner and start traveling around the world of SPLAH training your monsters and finding new ones and building the ultimate team!

  • Transform Your World

    BLAH uses your steps to power your actions in the game. Battle and capture other monstesr. Train and evolve your monsters. Compete and trade with your friends. Build the ultimate team! The world is in the power of your steps!

  • Travel the World of BLAH

    BLAH is a large world. Travel from the games town to find new... and stronger monsters!

  • 2 Different play styles

    Open the app, start a walk, and let the game run in the background. Or, help your monsters fight while you walk, we adapt to your play style!

Get Rewarded for a Healthy Lifestyle

Being active everyday is hard. BLAH makes it easier by giving you in-game points that'll help you get rare-unique monsters and more! All you have to do is make sure you achieve your daily goals!

  • Be Active, Get Rewarded

    BLAH is filled with milestones. Everytime you pass one. You get rewarded with rare monsters.

  • Team up with friends for more!

    Team up with your friends and combine your steps together to get even bigger rewards! Be healthy together!

Can You Collect Them All?

There's an ever-growing number of monsters with 100+ planned to be made. Each monster can evolve and have thier own unique ability and element. Assemble your personal team and take on the world!

  • Random Monsters

    By using in-game gems earned by being active, players unlock random unique monsters. No players will ever have the same experience!

  • Trade with your friends

    Your friend has a monster you want? Let's hope you have something they want too!
