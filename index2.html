Get Motivated

Make Walking Fun

Travel the world of Mobile Pals, where you create a team of Mobile Pals to take with you on your phone.

Encounter new and powerful Mobile Pals as you continue to explore the game world.

Use your steps to help fight against enemy Mobile Pals to earn coins, gems, and most importantly, more new Mobile Pals!

The more steps you take the more abilities you'll activate, the more rewards you'll get, and the more powerfuler you'll become!


  • Explore an ever-expanding universe with over 100+ Mobile Pals.
  • Each Mobile Pal has their own unique active and passive abilities that can turn the tides of the battle or help you gain more rewards! Create your strongest Mobile Pal team!
  • Train and evolve your Mobile Pals to form your ultimate dream team!
  • And of course! Low battery drain, guarenteed! Keep the app running in the background all day.

Get Rewards

The More You Walk the More You Get

Everyday, get rewarded with gems for achieving the daily doctor recommended 10,000 steps

Gems are you used to obtain unique powerful Mobile Pals that you can't find normally.

The more days in a row you complete, the better the rewards you get!

Want to build the ultimate team? It's easy. Stay fit and walk 10,000 steps everyday!


  • Get rewarded gems to unlock rare Mobile Pals.
  • Unlock keys to secret dungeons that'll allow you to level up and evolve your Mobile Pals faster!
  • Form parties with your friends to take on larger step milestones and share in the glory... or defeat!
  • ...And many more ways we'll reward you for being active!
